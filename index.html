Distributed Drone Control

Sep 2025 - Dec 2025

This project develops and tests a system where multiple robots can safely navigate around each other using a distributed control approach that divides the workspace into safe zones (Buffered Voronoi Cells) for each robot. The results show that this method completely eliminates collisions between robots across all test scenarios with 4 to 32 agents, though the robots take slightly longer, more conservative paths to reach their goals compared to more aggressive methods that occasionally allow near-misses or collisions.
